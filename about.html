About Us

About Join the Dots

Strengthening communities through empathy, dialogue, and conscious leadership.

Join the Dots is a community-rooted social enterprise dedicated to nurturing peace, compassion, and conscious connection in everyday life.

Inspired by the work of Marshall Rosenberg and the practice of Nonviolent Communication (NVC), we are a growing circle of trainers, certification candidates, facilitators, and listeners who believe that the way we speak and listen shapes the quality of our relationships, our leadership, and our communities.

We work to make compassionate communication accessible in Hindi, English, and regional Indian languages — so that empathy is not a luxury or a workshop concept, but a lived, daily possibility in homes, workplaces, institutions, and communities.

At Join the Dots, we create spaces where people can

  • Feel heard without judgement
  • Speak honestly without blame
  • Build boundaries with care
  • Navigate differences with dignity
  • Strengthen connection in personal and professional spaces

We are not simply running programmes.
We are nurturing a culture — one conversation at a time.

Our Vision

We envision communities across India where people feel heard, valued, and connected — where differences are met with dignity, dialogue bridges divides, and empathy shapes how we live, work, and lead.

Our Mission

We cultivate spaces for deep listening, honest dialogue, and shared learning — making compassionate communication accessible across languages and communities. Through structured practice, community-building, and leadership development, we nurture emotional resilience and relational intelligence so that empathy can be lived, not just learned.

A Living, Breathing Community

Join the Dots is not just a programme. It is a rhythm of shared practice.
We host free and paid spaces that welcome beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

Listening Circles

Free

We operate as a social enterprise, ensuring accessibility through scholarships and tiered offerings, while sustaining a professional, high-quality learning ecosystem.

Why We Exist

In a world where stress, speed, and misunderstanding touch every space — homes, workplaces, classrooms, and communities — communication is no longer a “soft skill.”

It is a life skill.

We believe peace is not built only in institutions or policies. It is built in living rooms. In classrooms. In boardrooms.

In the quiet courage to say, “Would you be willing to listen?”

In the tenderness to ask, “What matters most to you right now?”

And in the shared dream of asking, “How might we do this differently?”

Every time we choose dialogue to bridge distance, curiosity over judgement, and responsibility over blame — we participate in building a different kind of world.

About the Founder

Jayalakshmi (Jaya) Sengupta

NVC Certification Candidate · Community Builder · Trainer

Jayalakshmi (Jaya) Sengupta is a lifelong learner, development journalist, and recipient of the Laadli Media Award for gender-sensitive reporting. Her work has consistently centred on amplifying unheard voices, building bridges across differences, and nurturing dignity in dialogue.

In her post-retirement chapter, she founded Join the Dots as a collaborative platform dedicated to mainstreaming compassionate communication across India. Deeply rooted in Nonviolent Communication and enriched by mindfulness and counselling practices, Jaya designs and facilitates spaces that invite emotional literacy, conscious choice, and relational ease.

She is supported by a committed circle of highly trained facilitators and coaches who share a common longing: to create spaces that blend warmth, humour, structure, and depth, where people feel heard, held, and supported as they rediscover their own inner clarity and strength.
